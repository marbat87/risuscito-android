TWO ANGELS MET(*)

    Am  Dm      Am
Ch. Two angels met,
       Dm        Am
    one asked the other:
           Dm        Am       E       Am
    "Where is, where is the glory of God"?
           Dm        Am       E       Am
A.  "Where is, where is the glory of God"?
                  Dm
Ch. It is in the blessing,
                  Am
    it is in the blessing,
                  Dm
    it is in the blessing
          E       Am
    the glory of God.
                   Dm
A.  It is in the blessing,
                   Am
    it is in the blessing,
                   Dm
    it is in the blessing,
          E       Am
    the glory of God.

(*) The song can be repeated by rising a tone each time.
